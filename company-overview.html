WHO WE ARE

Psovic is a 100% employee-owned construction firm providing integrated construction services through a full range of delivery methods. We excel at projects of all sizes – from a single office fit-up to water supply programs valued at over $300 million.

We know what it takes to get things built and innovative solutions, talented people and specialized equipment top the list. But ensuring a successful project from start to finish takes even more – industry-leading safety practices, a multi- disciplined in-house estimating team, extensive self-perform capabilities and a fully integrated Special Projects Group, to name a few. PC has all this, and more, to ensure a seamless, cost-efficient process along the way and a satisfied client at the end.

Our Nigerian Content policy
We will act in a fair and ethical way. We will maintain a constructive dialogue with the Nigerian National Petroleum Corporation's Nigerian Content Division by consulting with and involving them throughout our tender processes. We will welcome and encourage Nigerian resources and suppliers to tender in those areas where competitiveness of cost, quality, delivery time and capacity IS demonstrated.
We will assist local suppliers through the establishment of programmes designed to help them meet tender criteria. We will train our Nigerian staff to meet the required worldwide industry standard

Our goal is to maximize Nigerian Content by involving capable local suppliers in our works and encouraging the local business community to develop resources that will continue to support our needs.

  • Construction --- Unmatched experience in the art and science of construction and construction management. We are experienced in bridge building, road construction, buildings and general civil works.
