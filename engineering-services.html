OUR DREDGING SERVICES OVERVIEW

Introduction to Dredging In a few words, dredging is essentially the underwater removal of sand, soil or silt and its movement from one place to another for purposes of deepening or making use of the removal material. Over the years, dredging has made a significant contribution to the development of many world economics. Construction and of harbors, canals, and waterways have all directly benefited from the dredging industry. Additionaly, dredging is key in coastal protection, land reclamation, and environmental restoration projects. Besides the above, there are many other applications to which dredging is key. In many place, agriculture depends on irrigation and drainage with the use of canals. Dredging is also often used for infrastructure projects such as road construction. Trenches for pipelines and cables and more, are often aided with the assistance of dredging as well.
Our Dredging services include: • Pond and River Dredging • Golf Course and Lake Dredging •Municipal and Industrial Lagoon Dredging • Channel Dredging • Harbor and Manna Dredging • Sand Dredging and Stockpiling • Navigation channels • Drainage canals • Environmental Dredging DREDGE CONTRACTING We provide contracting services in Commercial sand stockpiling for various customers. We also handle land reclamation and beach restoration projects. Dredging Project Consulting We provide design build consulting services, for project owners who need help planning and implementing a portable hydraulic dredging project. Our single source process allows owners to become part of a complete project team, without the responsibility of finding contractors, ordering surveys and applying for permits. Our dredging consulting services include: • Initial dredging project assessment and recommendations • Pre-dredging survey • Dredging project bid and specification writing • Assistance with obtaining dredging permits • Dredging budget and allocation recommendations
